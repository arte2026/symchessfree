# Privacy Policy for SymChess
**Effective Date:** [April 6 2026]
SymChess ("the app") is a chess game application developed and published by [Guillermo R Monroy]. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.
## 1. Information Collection SymChess does **not collect, store, or share any personal information** from users. The app does not require account creation and does not request personal data such as your name, email address, or location. ## 2. Local Data Storage The app may store data locally on your device for functionality purposes only, such as: * Game settings (e.g., selected variant, preferences) * Game progress or saved matches This data is stored solely on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers. ## 3. Internet Connectivity SymChess may be accessed via a web browser or installed as an app. The app itself does not send data over the internet. If hosted on a website, the hosting provider may collect standard technical data (such as IP address or browser type) as part of normal web operations. This is outside the control of the app. ## 4. Third-Party Services SymChess does **not use third-party analytics, advertising services, or tracking technologies**. ## 5. Children’s Privacy SymChess does not knowingly collect any personal information from children or users of any age. ## 6. Data Security Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is minimal risk related to data security. ## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" above. ## 8.
